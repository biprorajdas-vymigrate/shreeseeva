About

Visionary Leadership

Shree Seevanathan is an esteemed Principal Consultant with over a decade of experience in the legal services industry. Backed by a Juris Doctor (J.D.) and a Ph.D. in Law from the University of Southern Queensland, she has established herself as a leader in her field. Shree's expertise spans complex domains including Immigration Law, Startup Law, and Trademark Law, where she provides high-level strategic consulting.



Her distinguished career includes key roles as a Senior Policy Advisor for the Victorian Government, a Principal Consultant at Vymigrate Global Mobility, and Director-level positions in operations and international property. Recognized for her exceptional skills in negotiation, management, and strategic planning, Shree is dedicated to delivering strategic insights and practical solutions. Her passion for law and commitment to her clients drive her to achieve outstanding results in every undertaking.
